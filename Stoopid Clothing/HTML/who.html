Stoopid Clothing Company - Who We Are

Stoopid Clothing Co. is run by Casey Willett and Kylie Kaufman.

During our time working retail, we couldn’t help but notice the types of clothing people tended to gravitate toward, as well as the styles that people stayed away from. There are a lot of brands out there that charge way too much for starchy material with a plastic cocoon of graphics around it. Clothing should be comfortable and practical as well as affordable. People work hard for their money and should not be charged outrageous prices for sub-par materials.
